Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QRispr

Is this service really free?

Yes! QRispr is completely free for restaurants of all sizes. We offer secure hosting for your menu PDFs with no hidden fees or premium features.

How do I update my menu?

Simply return to QRispr and upload your updated PDF with the same title. Your QR code will automatically link to the new menu, so there's no need to reprint your QR codes.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support PDF format for menus. This ensures the best compatibility across all devices and a professional appearance for your customers.

Is there a limit to how many menus I can create?

No limits! You can create as many different QR codes as you need for different menus (lunch, dinner, drinks, specials, etc.).

How secure is the hosting for my menu?

We use UploadThing, a secure and reliable file hosting service with enterprise-grade security. Your menus are served over HTTPS for maximum security and reliability.
